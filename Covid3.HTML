College Education During a Pandemic

(CDC Updates on Covid-19)

With Covid-19 now having claimed the lives of over 250,000 Americans, descisions regardging in-person classes were made very carefully. This map shows that some of the hardest hit college campuses are on the East coast and in the South. The midwest has turned into the next large hotspot consistently setting new-case records for the past month. With multiple promising vaccine options coming out recently there is hope that life will return to normal, including in-person classes.
